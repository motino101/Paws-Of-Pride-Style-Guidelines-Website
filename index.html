PAWS OF PRIDE: STYLE GUIDE

LOGO

June, our character mascot, is the face of our brand. The image of June is our most recognisable brand asset. The preferred approach is to use the June logo by itself, separate from the wordmark. This allows flexibility to present June with greater prominence while maintaining a considered, open and modern presentation.
